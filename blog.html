I HAVEN'T BEEN EVERYWHERE BUT IT'S ON MY LIST

I must admit that this is my first blog post, this inspires me to share one of my experiences with you. What is best for our age are
the travels, research and carefree (to some extent) we carry on those trips. Looking for the best deals, using last minute offers,
collecting advice and recommendations from friends and sometimes going blind. There are no more mailboxes, now everything
is digitalized, the pictures are sent to viber messenger, we constantly post pictures to collect "likes" but also to brag about where
we have been. Here are some things that can not be left without

  • Mask
  • Pasport
  • Money (always take your credit carld like MasterCard or other paypall card)
  • Phone
  • Phone charger
  • Water
  • Medicines
  • Clothes
Avoid getting into trouble, google some of the laws in the country you are traveling to.Finally, EVERY TRAVEL IS A NEW EXPERIENCE
